---
title: "Staff"
description: "This is the sixth section of the workspace."
---

Within this section, you will be able to view all staff at **Kelva**, and <u>track their activity</u>, as well as <u>sessions</u>.

Additionally, you can click on each staff member individually to issue any <u>note</u>, <u>promotion</u>, <u>strike</u>, <u>demotion</u> or <u>termination</u>, to have them **logged** accordingly.

**Filters** can also be used to view just a <u>column</u> (e.g. Sessions Hosted).

<Warning>
  This section is only <u>visible</u> if you are part of the **Corporate Team** or _higher_.
</Warning>
