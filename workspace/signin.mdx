In order to access our workspace, you must verify and connect your Roblox account. This just takes less than 10 seconds!

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Sign Up">
    ### How do I connect my Roblox account?

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Accessing Website" icon="link">
        The first thing we need to know is how to access the **workspace**. Visit https://kelva.planetaryapp.cloud to enter the login page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="address-card">
        Once you access the website for the first time, you will need to click on the "**Sign Up**" tab, and afterwards, click on the last option: "**Sign Up with Roblox**".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verifying your Roblox Account" icon="badge-check">
        After clicking, a new tab will appear. Here, select the <u>Roblox account</u> you would like to use, and click "**Continue**".

        Then, you will have to give access to "**Kelva**", to be able to view your profile, and User ID. To do this, simply click on "**Confirm and Give Access**".

        Once done correctly, you will get **redirected** onto the workspace page. Great job!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Log In">
    ### How do I log in to my account?
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing Website" icon="link">
    First, access the website as normally, by going to https://kelva.planetaryapp.cloud.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="circle-user">
    Once you are on the website, click on "**Continue with Roblox**".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connecting your Roblox Account" icon="badge-check">
    After clicking, a new tab will appear. Here, select the <u>Roblox account</u> you would like to use, and click "**Continue**".

    Then, you will have to give access to "**Kelva**", to be able to view your profile, and User ID. To do this, simply click on "**Confirm and Give Access**".

    Once done correctly, you will get **redirected** onto the workspace page. Great job!
  </Step>
</Steps>
