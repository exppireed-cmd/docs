"**Home**" is the default section of the workspace, you will get used to it quickly. From here, you will be able to see <u>new onboarded staff</u>, and the <u>upcoming sessions</u>.

The first time you access this page, you will be able to set your **birthday**, to let anyone know, so they do not miss it!

### What it is for?

<Card title="New Staff" icon="clipboard-user" horizontal>
  See who has been recently registered!
</Card>

<Card title="Sessions" icon="calendar-lines" horizontal>
  View ongoing and upcoming sessions!
</Card>

<Note>
  You can change your <u>birthday</u> by going into your **account settings**!
</Note>
